About Enigma

Built on
competition
& respect

Enigma is a competitive Club Ultimate team based in Dayton, Ohio. We play to win, and we play for each other.

Whether you've played club, competed in college, or you're ready to step up from leagues & pickup — we want you on the line. Come to practices and try out.

We expect spirit of the game from everyone who wears the jersey, and we encourage our players to stay active in the wider Ultimate community year-round.
