About Enigma

Built on
competition
& respect

Enigma is a friendly and competitive Club Ultimate team based out of Dayton, Ohio. We pride ourselves on camaraderie, competition, and growth.

Whether you've played in club before, played on a college team, or you're ready to graduate from leagues & pickup — we want you here. Come out to practices and try out for the team.

We encourage players to remain active in the Ultimate community outside the club team, and demand mutual respect and spirit of the game from everyone.
